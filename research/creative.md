# Creative Agency Research: Parallax Creative

## 1. Color Theory & Examples
- **Primary Palette**: Neon Orange (#FF5F1F), Acid Green (#B0BF1A), and Off-Black (#111111).
- **Vibe**: Expressive, bold, unconventional, creative.
- **Example Sites**: Active Theory, Resn, Hello Monday.
- **Psychology**: High-contrast, "clashing" colors show confidence and creativity.

## 2. Animation & Style Inspiration
- **Key Animations**: 
  - Mouse-following cursor effects.
  - Distorted text on hover (Liquid distortion).
  - Infinite scroll project grids.
  - Magnetic buttons and interactive "play" areas.
- **Awwwards Inspiration**: "Studio Dumbar" (motion identity), "Wiedemann Lampe" (branding focus).

## 3. Media & Text Strategy
- **Media**: Experimental motion graphics, glitchy project previews, "behind the scenes" studio culture shots.
- **Typography**: Variable fonts that change weight/width on scroll.
- **Tone**: Playful, expert, disruptive.
- **Sample Copy**: "We don't follow trends. We invent the future of digital expression."
