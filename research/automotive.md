# Automotive Industry Research: Apex Dynamics

## Color Theory & Examples
- **Primary Palette:** Obsidian (#0A0A0A), Satin Chrome (#B4B4B4), Velocity Red (#E60000).
- **Psychology:** Speed, precision, engineering excellence, status, adrenaline.
- **Examples:** Porsche, Bugatti, Rimac, Singer Vehicle Design.

## Animation & Style Inspirations
- **Style:** High-tech, cinematic, performance-oriented. Dark modes with bright, glowing accents.
- **Animations:** "Power-on" sequences. GSAP for wheel rotation on scroll. Rapid-fire staggers. Mechanical-style masking (shutters/apertures).
- **Micro-interactions:** Gauge needle animations, carbon fiber texture shifts on hover, glowing digital buttons.

## Media & Text Usage
- **Media:** Close-ups of carbon fiber, engine components, and aerodynamic curves. High-speed tracking shots (video). Low-light studio photography.
- **Unsplash Keywords:** "supercar", "engine detail", "car wheel", "night driving", "carbon fiber".
- **Text:** Technical, bold, engineered. "ENGINEERED FOR THE UNCOMPROMISING", "THE ART OF VELOCITY", "PRECISION REIMAGINED".
