# Physiotherapy & Wellness Industry Research: Lumina Physio

## 1. Color Theory & Examples
- **Primary Palette**: Soft Sage Green (#89A894), Deep Navy (#1A2B3C), and Cloud White (#F8F9FA).
- **Vibe**: Clinical but calming, professional, fluid, human-centric.
- **Example Sites**: Foothills Physical Therapy, PhysioFlow, Comprehensive Healthcare Medical.
- **Psychology**: 
  - **Sage Green**: Growth, healing, and organic recovery.
  - **Deep Navy**: Trust, stability, and medical expertise.
  - **Cloud White**: Purity, cleanliness, and mental clarity.

## 2. Animation & Style Inspiration
- **Key Animations**: 
  - **Fluid Transitions**: Smooth, ease-in-out scroll animations mimicking the fluidity of healthy movement.
  - **Anatomical Overlays**: Subtle SVG path animations highlighting muscle groups or joints on hover.
  - **Range of Motion Sliders**: Interactive "before/after" sliders showing progress in flexibility or recovery.
  - **Floating Elements**: Soft, organic shapes that drift subtly in the background, avoiding harsh edges.
- **Awwwards Inspiration**: "Foothills Physical Therapy" (large imagery, clean layout), "PhysioFlow" (modern, premium clinical feel).

## 3. Media & Text Strategy
- **Media**: 
  - **Human-Centric**: Photography of active recovery, not just injury. 
  - **Tactile**: Close-ups of physical therapy techniques (hands-on care).
  - **Atmospheric**: Bright, sun-drenched clinic interiors that feel welcoming rather than sterile.
- **Typography**: 
  - **Headings**: Elegant Serif (e.g., Playfair Display or Lora) to convey empathy and history.
  - **Body**: Modern Sans-Serif (e.g., Inter or Plus Jakarta Sans) for clarity and readability.
- **Tone**: Empathetic, authoritative, and results-oriented.
- **Sample Copy**: "Precision Care for Every Move You Make. Restore your body's natural rhythm."
