# Fashion Industry Research: NOIR ATELIER

## 1. Color Theory & Examples
- **Primary Palette**: Stark Black (#000000), Pure White (#FFFFFF), and a single bold accent like Electric Blue (#0033FF) or Crimson (#DC143C).
- **Vibe**: Editorial, avant-garde, minimal, bold.
- **Example Sites**: Gucci, Balenciaga, Jacquemus.
- **Psychology**: Monochromatic palettes keep the focus entirely on the garment textures and colors.

## 2. Animation & Style Inspiration
- **Key Animations**: 
  - Horizontal scrolling lookbooks.
  - Glitch or "film grain" overlays.
  - Typography that "breaks" or stretches on scroll (GSAP ScrollTrigger).
  - Quick, snappy image transitions.
- **Awwwards Inspiration**: "Marine Serre" (digital forward), "Zara" (layout experimentation).

## 3. Media & Text Strategy
- **Media**: High-grain editorial photography, rapid-cut video loops of fabric movement.
- **Typography**: Bold, oversized Display fonts (e.g., Bodoni or custom Grotesk).
- **Tone**: Provocative, trend-setting, concise.
- **Sample Copy**: "Undefined by tradition. Redefined by the moment."
