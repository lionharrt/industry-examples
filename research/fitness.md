# Fitness Industry Research: Kinetic Performance Lab

## Color Theory & Examples
- **Primary Palette:** Electric Lime (#CCFF00), Deep Slate (#1A1A1A), Pure White.
- **Psychology:** Energy, motivation, high-intensity, precision, modern science.
- **Examples:** Nike Training, Gymshark, WHOOP.

## Animation & Style Inspirations
- **Style:** Brutalist/Grotesque. Heavy borders, large typography, "in-your-face" layouts.
- **Animations:** Fast, snappy transitions. GSAP Scrub for workout progress bars. Glitch effects on text. Parallax on athlete movement.
- **Micro-interactions:** Heartbeat pulses, vibrating buttons, counter animations.

## Media & Text Usage
- **Media:** Gritty, high-contrast photography of sweat, effort, and peak performance. Cinematic slow-motion video of training.
- **Unsplash Keywords:** "crossfit", "sweat athlete", "modern gym", "high intensity training", "neon fitness".
- **Text:** Direct, aggressive, results-oriented. "PUSH LIMITS", "ZERO EXCUSE", "PRECISION PERFORMANCE".
