# Real Estate Industry Research: Meridian Properties

## 1. Color Theory & Examples
- **Primary Palette**: Navy Blue (#000080), Champagne (#F7E7CE), and Slate Grey (#708090).
- **Vibe**: Trustworthy, expansive, high-end, aspirational.
- **Example Sites**: Sotheby's International Realty, Knight Frank, The Agency.
- **Psychology**: Blue evokes stability and trust; champagne accents add a touch of exclusivity.

## 2. Animation & Style Inspiration
- **Key Animations**: 
  - Property listing cards with hover-to-expand details.
  - Interactive floor plan reveals.
  - Smooth parallax on wide-angle architectural shots.
  - Stat counters for "Sq Ft", "Bedrooms", and "Market Value".
- **Awwwards Inspiration**: "One Thousand Museum" (luxury visuals), "79 Crosby" (minimalist elegance).

## 3. Media & Text Strategy
- **Media**: Drone shots of estates, interior "lifestyle" shots (sunlight hitting marble, pool ripples).
- **Typography**: Modern Sans-Serif (e.g., Inter or Roboto) for clarity and scale.
- **Tone**: Authoritative, visionary, exclusive.
- **Sample Copy**: "Curating the world's most exceptional addresses for those who appreciate the art of living."
