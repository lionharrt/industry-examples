# Boutique Hotel Industry Research: The Wanderer

## 1. Color Theory & Examples
- **Primary Palette**: Terracotta (#E2725B), Sage Green (#8A9A5B), and Sand (#C2B280).
- **Vibe**: Warm, experiential, serene, artisanal.
- **Example Sites**: Aman Resorts, Proper Hotels, Ace Hotel.
- **Psychology**: Earthy tones evoke relaxation and a connection to "place".

## 2. Animation & Style Inspiration
- **Key Animations**: 
  - Slow-zoom image galleries (Ken Burns effect).
  - Page transitions that feel like "turning a page" or "opening a door".
  - Floating weather/time widgets.
  - Smooth parallax on landscape photos.
- **Awwwards Inspiration**: "Soneva" (luxury experience), "The Ned" (classic/modern mix).

## 3. Media & Text Strategy
- **Media**: Wide-angle room shots, "POV" arrival videos, local craftsmanship details.
- **Typography**: Playful but refined Serif (e.g., Recoleta or Ogg).
- **Tone**: Welcoming, poetic, local.
- **Sample Copy**: "Where the rhythm of the tides meets the comfort of home. Your escape, redefined."
